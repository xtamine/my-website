Elden Ring

What is Elden Ring?

Elden Ring is an upcoming action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. The game is a collaborative effort between game director Hidetaka Miyazaki and fantasy novelist George R. R. Martin. It is scheduled for release on February 25, 2022 for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S.

Gameplay

Elden Ring is an action role-playing game played in third-person perspective. The player creates a custom character who can explore the game's open world, which is known as the Lands Between. The player can traverse the world on horseback and engage in combat with enemies using weapons such as swords and magic. The game features a day-night cycle and dynamic weather system that affects gameplay. The player can also summon spirits to assist them in combat. The game has a multiplayer component that allows players to join each other's games and engage in cooperative or competitive play.

Development

Elden Ring was announced at E3 2019. The game is a collaboration between game director Hidetaka Miyazaki and fantasy novelist George R. R. Martin. Miyazaki is known for his work on the Souls series of games, including Demon's Souls, Dark Souls, and Bloodborne. Martin is known for his A Song of Ice and Fire series of novels, which were adapted into the television series Game of Thrones. The game is being developed by FromSoftware, the studio behind the Souls series, and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. The game's world and lore were created by Martin, while Miyazaki designed the gameplay and mechanics. The game's title, Elden Ring, refers to a mysterious object that plays a central role in the game's story.
